Mobile Optimization: Don’t Miss the Boat

SEMrush reports that by 2025, approximately 70% of internet users will access the web via mobile devices. Google’s mobile-friendly update has significantly impacted search rankings. Use Google’s free mobile-friendly test to see how your site stacks up and make the necessary adjustments.

Google’s E-A-T Principles: Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness

Quality content is king, and Google’s E-A-T principles define what quality

means. Pages that adhere to these principles rank higher on Google. Algorithms like Google Panda and RankBrain play a crucial role in determining the quality of your content.

Featured Snippets: The Top Spot

According to Ahrefs, almost 99% of web pages shown in featured snippets already rank on SERP’s first page. To optimize for featured snippets, structure your content well, use eye-catching images, and answer multiple related questions in one article.

Image Optimization: A Picture is Worth a Thousand Clicks

SEMrush emphasizes the importance of image optimization in SEO. High-quality images not only enhance user experience but also contribute to better SEO. Make sure your images are optimized for speed and relevance.
